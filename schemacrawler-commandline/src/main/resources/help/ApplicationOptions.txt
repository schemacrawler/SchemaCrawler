--- Application Options ---

-loglevel=<loglevel>
  Sets the logging granularity, where <loglevel> is one of: 
  OFF, SEVERE, WARNING, INFO, CONFIG, FINE, FINER, FINEST, ALL
  Optional, default OFF

-?, -h, -help, --help
  Shows SchemaCrawler help

-V, --version
  Shows the SchemaCrawler version number
